Astrology

Astrology & Modern Horoscope Horoscope (astronomy) is derived from the Greek words horo (ώρα) meaning “hour, period or time zone” and skopus (σκοπός) meaning “focusing target or sign”. Astrology consists of the words astro (άστρο) meaning "star" and logia (λογια) meaning "study". The main idea of ​​such astrology in this modern horoscope is to mark the birth of an individual according to the constellations of the zodiac.
